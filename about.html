Designing with Purpose, Creating with Curiosity

Hey, I’m Kateryna—a UX/UI designer with a love for problem-solving, storytelling, and crafting digital experiences that just make sense.

My journey into design wasn’t exactly traditional. I started in journalism, chasing stories and uncovering insights, then moved into screenwriting, where I learned to shape narratives that resonate. But what really clicked for me was UX/UI design—a space where I could blend creativity with strategy, research with intuition, and storytelling with functionality.

I love digging deep into user behavior, asking the right questions, and shaping solutions that are not only visually compelling but genuinely useful. Whether it’s designing a seamless e-commerce experience, refining a brand’s digital presence, or building a tool that makes life easier, my goal is always the same: create with impact.

Right now, I’m expanding my skills in the Interaction Design Program at Capilano University, tackling real-world projects, collaborating in teams, and pushing myself to grow as a designer. I’ve worked on e-commerce platforms, brand websites, digital directories, and even a design hackathon—all with the goal of making digital experiences more intuitive, accessible, and engaging.

I’m looking for opportunities where I can bring my research-driven approach, creative problem-solving, and attention to detail to a team that values thoughtful, user-centered design. If you’re looking for someone who thrives on both the big picture and the small details, let’s connect!
